Description

2 Deep Learning Masterclasses

(Conference Room Setup)

About - info.deeplearningconf.com

July 26, 2017 - All-Day Masterclass - keywords - image recognition, fraud detection, anomaly detection, text analysis

1 - The Executing Deep Learning Strategies Masterclass (less technical)

Prerequisites: you should be comfortable with the words Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning.

Deep learning learns to make predictions about the future by crunching through vast quantities of existing data. Decisions based on deep learning can be both incredibly useful and have a profound impact on our lives. Since the decision-making criteria are essentially learned by the computer using artificial intelligence, rather than being pre-programmed by humans, careful implementation of these tools becomes an essential component of choosing to use them.

In this hands-on workshop, you will be introduced to a dozen applications for deep learning that are common in a production setting, and then to important methodologies for safe practice in organizing teams that deploy these solutions. Finally, you will leave with a solid, industry-standard framework for decision-making that a chief information/executive/technology officer can use to mitigate enterprise risk.


Morning: Introduction to deep learning - High-level overview of why enterprises are using deep learning

9:00 – 10:00 Introduction to machine learning and its applications

10:00 - 11:00 Introduction to deep learning and how it is currently being used by enterprises

11:00 - 12:00 Question and answer session with each student on deep learning's present or future role in their business

12:00-1:00 Lunch

Afternoon: Overview of deep learning strategies

1:00-2:00 Awareness building - educating specific groups within your enterprise on the maturity of the technology

2:00-3:00 Specialized to particular tasks - bespoke, custom, made-to-order solutions

3:00-4:00 Using deep learning across teams - capturing and re-using insights, running a deep learning-first enterprise

4:00-5:00 Question and answer session with each student on what their business would need out of a deep learning strategy



July 27, 2017 - All-Day Masterclass - keywords - TensorFlow, BigDL, Spark, HDFS, data augmentation

2 - The Deploying Enterprise Deep Learning Masterclass (more technical)

Prerequisites: you should be comfortable with the words Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Neural Networks, Training, and Algorithms.

As we keep pushing deep learning technology forward, a strong connection between researchers and implementers is crucial to get the best results. An entire field exists, called deep learning engineering or deep learning systems, that looks at the whole lifecycle of a deep learning solution, all the way from initial research through to deployment in production. This integrated view is essential to delivering cost-effective solutions that work within the constraints of your budget.

In this hands-on workshop, you will be introduced to enterprise deep learning frameworks and solutions that deliver competitive results at scale. Learning from companies like Google and Facebook, who have been executing effective deep learning deployment strategies for 5+ years, you will be exposed to technologies that can integrate with your stack and software architectures that are used in production by enterprises like Intel, Microsoft, Amazon, and Apple.


Morning: Introduction to deep learning - Overview of deep learning frameworks in use

9:00 - 10:00 Refresher on machine learning, deep learning in the enterprise setting

10:00 - 11:00 High level overview of deep learning frameworks and their enterprise users

11:00 - 12:00 Question and answer session with each student on deep learning engineering's present or future role in their business

12:00 - 1:00 Lunch

Afternoon: Overview of deploying enterprise deep learning

1:00 - 2:00 When to deploy deep learning - where deep learning does exceptionally well

2:00 - 3;00 Deep learning systems - presentation of deep learning architectures that other companies have used

3:00 - 4:00 Your deep learning environment - Integrating models with your data, where your data already lives

4:00 - 5:00 Question and answer session with each student on what their business would need out of a deep learning system


Details

Both Masterclass events from Deep Learning Conf™ will be held at Rise NY - www.thinkrise.com/newyork.html - on July 26, 9am - 5pm, and July 27, 9am - 5pm, and are put on by Enterprise Deep Learning -www.enterprisedeeplearning.com.

Speaker


Sam Putnam is CEO/Founder, and a Deep Learning Consultant, at Enterprise Deep Learning, LLC. He directs machine learning projects, solves clients' business problems, and trains deep neural networks on large datasets. Sam is also a contributor to the TensorFlow machine learning project and a member of the Machine Learning Society.

Sam previously worked at the Dartmouth Center for Imaging Medicine, the National Renewable Energy Laboratory, and the Lab for Cognition and Control in Complex Systems.
